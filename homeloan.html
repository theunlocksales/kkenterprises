HOME LOAN FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum loan amount for home purchase?

You can get home loans up to â‚¹10 crores or 90% of the property value (whichever is lower). The exact amount depends on your income, existing liabilities, credit score, and property value.

What tax benefits do I get on home loans?

You can claim up to â‚¹1.5 lakhs deduction on principal repayment under Section 80C and up to â‚¹2 lakhs on interest payment under Section 24(b). First-time homebuyers get additional â‚¹50,000 under Section 80EEA.

Can I get a home loan for under-construction property?

Yes, home loans are available for under-construction properties from approved builders. The loan is disbursed in stages based on construction progress. You pay only interest during construction period; EMI starts after possession.

What is the processing time for home loan?

With complete documentation, home loan approval takes 5-7 working days. Technical and legal verification of property takes another 3-5 days. Total disbursal time is typically 10-15 working days.

Can I prepay my home loan without penalty?

Most banks do not charge prepayment penalty on floating rate home loans. For fixed rate loans, some banks may charge 2-3% prepayment charges. We help you choose loans with minimal or no prepayment penalties.
