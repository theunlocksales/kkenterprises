What is the maximum loan amount I can get?

How quickly can I get the loan disbursed?

With complete documentation, loan approval happens within 48 hours. Disbursement is done within 24 hours of final approval directly to your bank account.

Is any collateral required for personal loan?

No, personal loans are unsecured loans that don't require any collateral. The loan is sanctioned based on your income, credit score, and employment stability.

Can I prepay my personal loan?

Yes, you can prepay your personal loan anytime. Most lenders don't charge prepayment penalties. However, it's recommended to check with your specific lender's prepayment policy.

What documents are needed for personal loan?

Basic documents include Aadhaar & PAN card, salary slips (last 3 months), bank statements (last 6 months), address proof, and passport size photographs. Self-employed individuals need ITR and business proof.
