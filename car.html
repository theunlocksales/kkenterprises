CAR LOAN FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I finance 100% of the car's on-road price?

Yes, many lenders offer up to 100% financing on the on-road price including registration, insurance, and accessories. However, it depends on your credit profile and income. Typically, 85-90% financing is standard.

What's the age limit for used car loans?

Most lenders finance used cars up to 10 years old. The total age of the car (current age + loan tenure) typically should not exceed 12-15 years at the end of the loan period.

How quickly can I get car loan approval?

With complete documentation, in-principle approval happens within 48 hours. After car inspection (for used cars) and final documentation, disbursal takes 2-4 working days.

Can I transfer my car loan to another lender?

Yes, car loan balance transfer is possible if you find a better interest rate. You need to pay foreclosure charges (if any) to your current lender and processing fees to the new lender.

Can I prepay my car loan?

Yes, you can prepay your car loan partially or fully anytime. Most banks charge 2-5% prepayment penalty, while some NBFCs allow free prepayment after 6 months. Check your lender's specific prepayment policy.
